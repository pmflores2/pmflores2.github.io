GradMAP Philippines

STEM Mentorship Network

I am a volunteer mentor for GradMAP Philippines, supporting its mission to make STEM careers and education more accessible to Filipino students. GradMAP increases the number of Filipinos pursuing advanced STEM degrees by offering one-on-one mentorship, pairing students with experienced professionals to guide them through the graduate school application process and refine their materials.
