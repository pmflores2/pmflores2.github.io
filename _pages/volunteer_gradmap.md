#### **GradMAP Philippines**
##### *STEM Mentorship Network*

I am a volunteer mentor for <a href="https://www.gradmap.ph/">GradMAP Philippines</a>, supporting its mission to make STEM careers and education more accessible to Filipino students. GradMAP increases the number of Filipinos pursuing advanced STEM degrees by offering one-on-one mentorship, pairing students with experienced professionals to guide them through the graduate school application process and refine their materials.
