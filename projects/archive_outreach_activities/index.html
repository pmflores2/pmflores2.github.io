GradMAP Philippines
STEM Mentorship Network
I am a volunteer mentor for GradMAP Philippines, supporting its mission to make STEM careers and education more accessible to Filipino students. GradMAP increases the number of Filipinos pursuing advanced STEM degrees by offering one-on-one mentorship, pairing students with experienced professionals to guide them through the graduate school application process and refine their materials.
Pinoy Scientists
Spotlighting Pinoy Scientists, one post at a time
Pinoy Scientists highlights the lives of Filipino scientists through weekly Instagram and Facebook takeovers. The platform serves as an inspiring role model for the next generation of Filipinos. I took over the page from October 29 to November 4, 2023.

Philippine Science High School
Western Visayas Campus
The Philippine Science High School (PSHS) system is a network of specialized public high schools dedicated to nurturing the country's future leaders in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). It currently has 16 campuses around the country, with my alma mater being the Western Visayas Campus. I gave a webinar on October 3, 2023, to share my experience in pursuing a career in physics research.
